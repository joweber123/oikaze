You're special and so are our tours

Commiting the time and resources to a dream vacation in Japan can be a challenge, yet it’s guaranteed to be rewarding.

Oikaze trip culture

There are otherworldly treasures around every corner in Japan, and it can be daunting trying to narrow your plans down to your time frame. Our process helps to guide you through your lists of “must-see,” “want-to,” and “hope-for.” Many people start with a basic Japan bucket list, so we can tailor a tour around that starter list. Others start like we did; a total blank canvas and open to suggestions. Either way, we will work with you to create a unique experience tailored to your input and expectations. Of course there are limitations. The limits of time and money can actually help narrow our search for your perfect tour. Luckily there isn’t one ideal vacation, and we have ideas and choices for every week of the year and can accommodate (nearly) any budget.

Booking with us is personal

We aren’t going to send you a catalog, or a description page because the adventure you are booking can only be written in YOUR diary. Let’s have a conversation and make a plan that fits your ideals, your goals, and your budget. We have the time, the flexibility and the vision to meet your needs while also providing our own ideas and suggestions. The planning phase is not only necessary, but one of the best parts of an adventure! You just wait… you’ll be googling “Onsen” during office hours. You’ll see
