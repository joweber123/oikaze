Why Us?

Some of us grew up here, some of us came and found our hearts here. All of us love this country and want to share the hidden wonders and beauty that Japan has to offer.

We will plan the best possible trip for you. People have differing goals and interests, and cyclotourists are no different. Our focus is one of personalization so that everyone can have their adventure -- on their terms. Because we take the time and effort, the rewards are a deeper understanding of the land, its culture, the people, and ourselves and our place on this planet.

Why Japan?

Japan is one of the most unique countries on earth, both in culture and natural beauty. The mountains are rugged, but the roads are well-maintained. The history is ancient, but the technology appears from the future. All of this, along with a hot-spring soak at the end of every day make Japan a perfect destination for any adventurer. Oikaze takes you into this awe-inspiring countryside where mountain meets sea on roads waiting to be discovered.

“You’ll feel out of this world, but never out of place.”
